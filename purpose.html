Purpose



To inform the employer about your education, work experience, skills and interests. To ‘sell’ these qualities and to persuade the employer to invite you to interview. What should be on a CV? Start with contact information • Full name • Permanent mailing address • E-mail address that won’t expire • Phone numbers • Education • Honors and Awards • Professional Experience (employment) • Publications and presentations • Extracurricular and volunteer experience

Other sections for a CV




• Certifications and licensure • Professional affiliations • Professional activities • Research • Added qualifications. I also prepared a CV draft for you. This is a standard CV. You can fill this CV yourself. In this way, you will prepare your first CV.
