Important Points



There are four main things employers will look at in CVs: • Education • Ability to think clearly • Analyse and assess information • Draw conclusions • Work independently • Research. • Work experience • Ability to get on with people • Work under pressure • Meet deadlines. • Leisure interests • Ability to plan and organize • Co-operate with others • Compete • Lead • Work hard to achieve results. • Specific skills.

Examples




Driving license, computer skills, foreign languages, artistic skills. There is no need to list every job you’ve ever had, detail the most relevant.
